WELCOME

At The Dojo and Fit Club, we help our students to reach their fitness goals through training in the martial arts, we build confidence and leadership skills in our youth, and we encourage the development of respect, discipline, and character from every one of our martial artists. We offer a venue for many various fitness goals: tournament competition, self-defense, weight loss, and general fitness. Our programs cultivate a community of people drawn together by the desire to train.
